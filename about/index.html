About me

Hey there, first of all thanks for checking out my site, it's my own little slice of the internet to show off who I am and what I do.

I started getting into coding at the start of the COVID-19 pandemic in early 2020. The first language I learned was C++. I spent a lot of time with it and I feel that C++, while definitely a difficult language to learn without prior experience, was an excellent way to get a solid grasp on a lot of coding concepts that make other languages that much easier to work with.

Since then I've taken CodeCademy courses for C++, C#, Javascript and Python. I've taken courses on Ruby through The Odin Project. I've also taken web development courses for HTML and CSS through the BitWise Workforce Training program.

All in all I've done a good amount of self-study and solo projects involving back-end development, and am now enjoying team-based collaborative full-stack learning through the BitWise community.

I'm always up for a new challenge, if you have a project that you'd like to collaborate on or if you just want to talk code, feel free to pop on over to the Contact section and shoot me an email.
